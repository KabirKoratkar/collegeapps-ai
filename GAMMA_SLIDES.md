## Slide 1: The Problem
**Card Title:** The College Admissions Crisis: Chaos & Inequality
**Layout Suggestion:** Split screen (Text on left, Image of chaotic spreadsheet/stressed student on right)

**Bulleted List / Key Points:**
*   **Overwhelming Complexity:** The average student juggles **10-15 applications**, managing over **40 unique deadlines** and **20+ essay prompts** across disconnected portals.
*   **The Equity Access Gap:** High-quality private counseling costs **$200-$500/hour**, leaving the majority of students to rely on overworked public school counselors with caseloads of **400+ students**.
*   **Tool Fragmentation:** Students currently manage the most important project of their lives using fragile, disconnected systems: manual spreadsheets, scattered Google Docs, and sticky notes.
*   **The Result:** Missed deadlines, generic essays, and massive student burnout.
