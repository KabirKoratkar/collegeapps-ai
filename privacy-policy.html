Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

At CollegeApps.ai, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your college application journey is secure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when creating an account, filling out your profile, or using our AI Counselor:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile details (e.g., graduation year, intended major).
  • Application Data: The colleges you are interested in, your task lists, deadlines, and essay drafts.
  • Uploaded Documents: Transcripts, resumes, and other documents you store in your Document Vault.
  • AI Interactions: Conversations with our AI Counselor and the research findings generated for you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Personalizing your application dashboard and task lists.
  • Powering the AI Counselor to provide personalized advice and research.
  • Enabling autosave and version control for your essays.
  • Providing technical support and responding to your inquiries.
  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve the user experience for all students.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (Postgres) and Supabase Storage. We implement Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure that only you can access your personal application data and documents.

4. Shared Content

If you choose to use our sharing features (e.g., sharing an essay for review), we will share that specific content with the recipient you designate. We do not share your private data with third parties unless required by law or to provide the specific services you have requested (e.g., OpenAI for AI Counselor functionality).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can manage most of your data through your settings and dashboard. To request a full account deletion, please contact our support team.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@collegeapps.ai.
