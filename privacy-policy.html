Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

At Waypoint, your privacy is a priority. This policy outlines what data we collect and how we use it to help you succeed in your college applications.

1. Data Collection

We collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account Data: Email, name, and profile information.
  • Application Data: Your college list, essay drafts, and tasks.
  • AI Counseling: History of your interactions with the AI Counselor to provide better advice.

2. Data Usage

Your information is used solely to:

  • Personalize your application dashboard.
  • Generate AI-powered insights and task lists.
  • Enable essay autosaving and document storage.

3. Document Security

Documents uploaded to the Document Vault (e.g., transcripts or resumes) are stored in secure buckets with strict access controls. Only you can access your private files.

4. Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI for AI functionality and Supabase for secure data storage. We do not sell your personal data to advertisers.

5. Your Control

You can edit or delete your data at any time through your dashboard. For full account deletion, please contact support.
